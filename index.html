Kingdom Of Eswatini

This is the beautiful Kingdom of Eswatini where people that live there are referred to as Emaswati. It is located in the southern part of Africa where it is surrounded by South Africa, and Mozambique thus it is a landlocked country. This country is known for it's beauty and magnificent landscape, with game reserves such as the Hlane Game Reserve.
